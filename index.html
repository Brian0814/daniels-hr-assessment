How to use this tool

Purpose & Approach

This Talent & Culture Self-Assessment is designed to help your organization reflect on current people practices across key HR and organizational culture areas. The purpose is to identify strengths, surface gaps, and inform future investments or strategic initiatives to build a stronger and more equitable talent ecosystem.

There are no right or wrong answers — the goal is clarity and insight, not perfection. Complete this assessment as honestly as possible. You may use this tool collaboratively; doing so can spark rich internal dialogue that aligns leadership and HR around shared priorities.

Rating scale

After reviewing the reflection questions under each sub-area, rate your organization's current state using the four-point scale below. Select the option that most honestly reflects where your organization is today, not where you aspire to be.

1
No practice in place
This area has not been addressed or formalized
2
Early stage / Informal
Some activity exists but is largely ad hoc
3
Documented but inconsistent
Written practices exist but aren't reliably applied
4
Consistently applied
Fully implemented, effective, and regularly used
